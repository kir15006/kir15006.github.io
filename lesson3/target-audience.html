Target Audience

The target audience of this site is both current residents as well as visitors to east Idaho. These will be people who are looking for both the weather forcast, as well as up to date and accurate information on current events and news for the area.

Personas

One example of someone who will visit this site is Dalton, a resident of Preston Idaho, he is a soccer coach and needs to know the weather for the week so he can know when to plan practices and games. He may also be looking to announce events such as games or tryouts to the community.

Another example of someone who will visit this site is Kaylee, a visitor from out of town. She is going to be in soda springs for a few days and is looking for something to do while she is in town. She is particularly intterested in any hotsprings in the area that she could enjoy a relaxing soak in.

Scenarios

  • Is it going to rain at all this weekend? I am planning on going on a hike.
  • Is a rodeo or similar event planned for this month?
  • How is the local high shcool football team doing?
  • What hotsprings are closed becasue of covid 19?
  • What is the average temperature around May in Preston?
