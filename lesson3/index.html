East Idaho Forcast

Domain: eastidahoforecast.com

Purpose:

The purpose of this website is to provide residents and visitors of eastern Idaho access to not only the weather forcast, but local news and events. They will be able to easily and quickly acess the weather forcast for several towns in east idaho. They will also be able to keep up with the events and news in those towns to allow them to make plans with up to date and accurate information.
